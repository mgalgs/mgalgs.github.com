h4. Overview

WikiPaper is a live wallpaper for Android that displays random
Wikipedia articles on your home screen. It's available for download "from the Amazon Market":http://www.amazon.com/Mitchel-Humpherys-WikiPaper/dp/B005WG44KY/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1322721969&sr=8-1.

Also, the source code is availabe "on GitHub":https://github.com/mgalgs/wikipaper.
